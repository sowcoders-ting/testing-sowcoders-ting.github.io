Helping Homelessness

Mission Statement

To provide services to those experiencing homelessness to get them settled in a stable situation, so they can feel safe and secure enough to get on the path towards recovery.

Services

  • Donation acceptance area
  • Shelter area, equipped with:
    • Bedding
    • Blankets
    • Pillows
    • Bathrooms
    • Helpful Services
      • Medical staff
      • Haircuts
